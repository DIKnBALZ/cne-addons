Put all your YCE Character !DIRECTORIES! into `CharInput`.

if script is not in the assets folder, go into `YCEtoCodename.hx` and changes the paths to your current paths.

When thats finished, go to the main menu and press 6.

All your converted characters will be in `CharOutput`.

All converted characters are formated to be easily drag and dropped.

*An example BF is included*
